title: The Augmented Mind Podcast
---

**The AM Podcast** focuses on topics around technical human-centered AI work. 

The AM Podcast is a student-led effort. The hosts include [Yijia Shao](https://cs.stanford.edu/~shaoyj/), [Shannon Shen](https://www.szj.io/) and [Michael Ryan](https://michryan.com/) from Stanford and MIT.
